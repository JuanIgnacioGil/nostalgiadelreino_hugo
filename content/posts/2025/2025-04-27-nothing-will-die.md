The world was never made;<br>  
It will change, but it will not fade<br> 
So let the wind range;  <br>
For even and morn <br>
Ever will be<br>
Thro' eternity<br>
Nothing was born;<br>
Nothing will die;<br>
All things will change.<br>

**Alfred, Lord Tennyson**
