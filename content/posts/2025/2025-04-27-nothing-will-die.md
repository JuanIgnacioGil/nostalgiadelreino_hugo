The world was never made;</p>  
It will change, but it will not fade</p> 
So let the wind range;  </p>
For even and morn </p>
Ever will be</p>
Thro' eternity</p>
Nothing was born;</p>
Nothing will die;</p>
All things will change.</p>

**Alfred, Lord Tennyson**
